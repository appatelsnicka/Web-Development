Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Medmoiselle Caroline Reivère was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean August Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100x70 cm
Home: Musèe du Louvre, Paris
